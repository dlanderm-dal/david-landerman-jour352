Bootstrap 5 Carousel with Text Boxes

First Slide Title

This is your custom text content. You can put anything here â€” paragraphs, lists, buttons, icons, whatever you want. No image required!

Second Slide Title

Each slide is just a div with your content inside. Style it however you like with your own CSS classes.

Third Slide Title

The key is setting a min-height on your content div so the carousel has a consistent size.

THE STRUCTURE
<!-- Your wrapper div -->
<div class="carousel-wrapper">

  <!-- Bootstrap carousel -->
  <div id="textCarousel" class="carousel slide" data-bs-ride="carousel">
    
    <!-- Indicators (optional) -->
    <div class="carousel-indicators">
      <button data-bs-target="#textCarousel" data-bs-slide-to="0" class="active"></button>
      <button data-bs-target="#textCarousel" data-bs-slide-to="1"></button>
      <button data-bs-target="#textCarousel" data-bs-slide-to="2"></button>
    </div>

    <!-- The slides -->
    <div class="carousel-inner">

      <!-- Slide 1 (must have .active on first one!) -->
      <div class="carousel-item active">
        <div class="text-slide">  <!-- YOUR CONTENT BOX -->
          <h2>Title</h2>
          <p>Your content here...</p>
        </div>
      </div>

      <!-- Slide 2 -->
      <div class="carousel-item">
        <div class="text-slide">
          ...
        </div>
      </div>

    </div>

    <!-- Prev/Next controls -->
    <button class="carousel-control-prev" data-bs-target="#textCarousel" data-bs-slide="prev">
      <span class="carousel-control-prev-icon"></span>
    </button>
    <button class="carousel-control-next" data-bs-target="#textCarousel" data-bs-slide="next">
      <span class="carousel-control-next-icon"></span>
    </button>

  </div>
</div>
THE CSS (your custom text box)
.text-slide {
  /* IMPORTANT: Set a min-height so carousel has consistent size */
  min-height: 300px;
  padding: 2rem;
  
  /* Center content vertically */
  display: flex;
  flex-direction: column;
  justify-content: center;
  
  /* Your styling */
  background: linear-gradient(135deg, #667eea 0%, #764ba2 100%);
  border-radius: 8px;
  color: white;
}
