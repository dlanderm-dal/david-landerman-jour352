Heat Map Themes

Thermal imaging palettes applied to a resume site

Heat maps use color gradients to show intensity — cool blues for low values, warm reds/yellows for high.
These themes borrow those associations: the accent color is the "hot spot" that draws your eye,
while the rest stays cool and neutral. Each interprets the heat map concept differently.

1 — Classic Thermal
The standard infrared camera look: deep navy cool zone, hot red accent, amber/yellow for highlights. Section headers glow hot.
